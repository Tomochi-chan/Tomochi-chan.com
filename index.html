The Problem

Our kids are growing up
inside a screen.

Scroll. Autoplay. Repeat. The feeds are designed to hold attention — and they're working. Kids are spending more time passively consuming content than ever before, and less time exploring, playing, and actually feeling things.

This isn't about banning screens. It's about giving kids something better to reach for.

"This wasn't the childhood we imagined for them."
